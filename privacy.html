Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Moondance is an astrophotography planning app developed by Peter F. Wingard ("See the Show"). This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Moondance collects minimal data to function:

  • Location data — If you tap "Use GPS," the app requests your approximate location to determine your observation site coordinates and timezone. This data is used entirely on your device for astronomical calculations. It is never transmitted to any server.
  • App preferences — Your selected location, target, and observation settings are saved on your device using standard iOS storage so they persist between sessions.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information, names, or email addresses (unless you voluntarily send a bug report).
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services in the iOS app.
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.
  • We do not use cookies or advertising identifiers.

Bug Reports

The app includes an optional bug report feature that opens your device's email client. If you choose to send a report, it includes basic device information (model, iOS version, app version) and your current app settings to help diagnose issues. This information is sent directly from your email account to our support address. We do not store this data beyond what is needed to address the reported issue.

Third-Party Services

The iOS app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All astronomical calculations are performed locally on your device.

Data Storage

All app data (your preferences, selected locations, and targets) is stored locally on your device. No user data is stored on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Moondance does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at seetheshow87@gmail.com.
